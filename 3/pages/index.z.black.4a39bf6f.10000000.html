Step 4: Add a particular folder to your computer's PATH environment variable

Search for "path" and click "Edit the system environment variables".

Click the "Environment Variables..." button.

In the "System variables" panel, click the line for "Path" and then click the "Edit..." button. Click the "New" button and then type "c:deafarmityorkshopmd". Replace "c:" with the letter of whatever device has your ideafarm.home.1 folder.

Click the "OK" button for each of the re dialog boxes to close them all.
