Step 7: Edit settings to tell Windows that you do not want it to launch IPDOS (tm) automatically

By default, the IPDOS (tm) production home will launch automatically when Windows starts up. To change this, search for "services", open the "Services" app, and find the line for "IdeaFarm (tm) Piggyback Distributed Operating System". Right click on it and then click on "Properties". Change the "Startup type" to "Manual" and then click the "OK" box to close the dialog. Click the X at top right to close the Services app.
